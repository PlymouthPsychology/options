Stage 1

Single honours students take all six modules listed below. Combined honours students take modules marked * as well as one 20-credit module per semester in their minor subject.

Semester 1

PSYC421: Cognitive Psychology*

PSYC422: Clinical and Developmental Psychology*

PSYC423: Environmental Psychology

Semester 2

PSYC424: Social Psychology*

PSYC425: Perception and the Brain*

PSYC426: Motivation and Performance

Note for students in Stage 2 and above: Stage 1 has been redesigned for 2024/25. If you studied Stage 1 prior to this year then you will have taken a different set of modules.
